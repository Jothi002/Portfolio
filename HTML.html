    2D Design

    Most of drafting is done in 2D design. Otherwise, designs for layout and drawing preparation are laser-cut.Drafting is used for creating part drawings, subassembly drawings, and assembly drawings.The tolerance and geometric dimension symbols are mentioned in the part drawing and some assembly drawings.Following the design of the sheet metal components, a folding drawing is created and the components are assembled for laser cutting.Electrical panel layout designs, electrolyte drawings for machines and fixtures, and pneumatic circuit drawings for fixtures and machines are instances of layout designs.
